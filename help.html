Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Browse products, add to cart, and proceed to checkout.
Can I pay on delivery?
Yes, we support Pay on Delivery for selected locations.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes 1–3 working days depending on your location.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.
Do you accept returns?
Yes, returns are accepted within 3 days of delivery.
How do I track my order?
Go to your Orders page to view current order status.
What if I receive a wrong item?
Contact support immediately and we will replace it.
Is my payment secure?
Yes, all payments are processed securely.
Can I order multiple items?
Yes, you can add multiple items to your cart.
Do I need an account to order?
Yes, you need to be logged in to place an order.
How do I contact support?
Use the Inbox or WhatsApp button in your account page.
Can I change my delivery address?
Yes, before placing your order during checkout.
Why is my order delayed?
Delays may happen due to logistics or high demand.
Do you offer discounts?
Yes, promotions are available occasionally.
Can I reorder previous items?
Yes, from your Order History page.
What if my payment fails?
Try again or choose another payment method.
How do I reset my password?
Use the forgot password option on login page.
